# NotebookLM Opportunity Intelligence Extraction

## Objective
Convert notebook-derived business insights into operational opportunity pipelines.

## Extraction Domains
- Business opportunities
- AI automation offers
- Affiliate opportunities
- Low-cost startup concepts
- Content opportunities
- Monetization strategies

## Routing Targets
- Opportunity Hall of Fame
- Revenue Engine
- Today in Nexus
- Hermes recommendations

## Current State
- Revenue/Hall of Fame systems exist.
- Direct NotebookLM-to-opportunity routing is partial and mostly manual/heuristic.

## Completion Actions
1. Add opportunity extraction template from notebook summary lines.
2. Score opportunity by feasibility, time-to-value, and monetization confidence.
3. Store as proposed opportunities with dedup and source traceability.
4. Surface top approved opportunities in Today in Nexus and Hermes daily brief.

## Monetization Loop Example
NotebookLM opportunity -> Hall of Fame candidate -> Hermes recommendation -> content CTA -> lead magnet -> onboarding -> newsletter -> subscription path.

## Trust Controls
- Review-required before user-facing promotion.
- No automated outbound spam actions.
