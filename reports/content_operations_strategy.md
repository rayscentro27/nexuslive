# Content Operations Strategy

Date: 2026-05-11

## Operating Model
- Weekly sprint cadence with daily publish windows.
- Single source of truth: content queue states and asset manifests.
- Roles: researcher, script operator, editor, publisher, analyst.

## Core Workstreams
1. Trend Tracking
- Daily trend capture from approved channels and internal opportunity signals.

2. Hook Library
- Maintain tested hook categories: curiosity, urgency, myth-break, checklist, case-study.

3. Script Production
- Standardized templates by format and audience sophistication level.

4. Asset Planning
- Thumbnail and caption planning integrated before editing starts.

5. Distribution
- Platform-adapted packaging with consistent CTA hierarchy.

6. Learning Loop
- Weekly retro on winners/losers with next-week topic bias update.

## KPI Stack
- Top-of-funnel: impressions, CTR, hook hold (3-sec retention).
- Mid-funnel: completion, save/share rate.
- Bottom-funnel: click-through to Nexus pages, invite signups, qualified lead starts.

## Step-Linked Expansion Items
- Revenue opportunity queue tied to content topics.
- MVP launch prompts generated from top-performing educational themes.
- Internal-first intelligence remains default when generating narratives.
