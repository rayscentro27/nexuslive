## Revenue Opportunity Taxonomy

```
CATEGORY A — AI SERVICES (Fastest path to revenue)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Consulting:   Help businesses implement AI tools/workflows
  Automation:   Build recurring automations for clients (retainer)
  Auditing:     AI readiness audits — quick project, high value
  Training:     Teach AI tools to business owners / teams
  
  Revenue model: Hourly → Project → Monthly retainer
  Time to first $: Days to weeks
  Capital required: $0 (knowledge-based)

CATEGORY B — AI PRODUCTS (Medium-term, recurring)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  SaaS tools:    Lightweight AI tools solving a niche problem
  Prompt packs:  Sell packaged prompts / systems for specific industries
  Templates:     Notion/Airtable/automation templates powered by AI
  Newsletters:   Paid AI intel newsletter for a specific vertical
  
  Revenue model: One-time or subscription
  Time to first $: Weeks to months
  Capital required: Low ($0–$500 for tools)

CATEGORY C — AI GRANTS + FUNDING (Strategic, non-dilutive)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  SBIR/STTR:     Federal grants for AI research applications
  Foundation:    Grants for AI + social impact, workforce, education
  Accelerators:  AI-focused programs (equity trade — evaluate carefully)
  Credits:       AWS/Google/Azure AI startup credits (free compute)
  
  Revenue model: Non-dilutive capital
  Time to first $: 30–180 days depending on grant
  Capital required: Proposal writing time only

CATEGORY D — AI-POWERED EXISTING SERVICES (Leverage Nexus)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Enhance existing offerings with AI (speed, personalization, scale)
  Use Hermes/Nexus infrastructure as a competitive advantage
  Automate back-office → redirect time to revenue
```

## AI Consulting Fast-Launch Playbook

```
FASTEST PATH TO FIRST AI REVENUE (30-Day Sprint)

WEEK 1 — IDENTIFY
  □ List 5 businesses in your network that are behind on AI
  □ Score each with the opportunity scorecard
  □ Pick top 2 for outreach
  □ Define your "AI Starter Package" offer (scope + price)

  OFFER IDEA — AI STARTER AUDIT:
  ┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
  │  "AI Business Readiness Audit"                      │
  │  What you get:                                      │
  │  • 1-hour discovery call                            │
  │  • Map of 5 processes AI can automate today         │
  │  • Prioritized implementation roadmap               │
  │  • Tool recommendations (no fluff)                  │
  │  Price: $497–$997 (start at $497 to close faster)  │
  │  Delivery: 5 business days                          │
  └─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

WEEK 2 — OUTREACH
  □ Direct message to 2 warm contacts (not cold email yet)
  □ Frame: "I'm helping businesses audit their AI opportunities — 
            interested in a free 30-min consult?"
  □ Goal: 1 booked call by end of week
  □ Prepare discovery call questions

WEEK 3 — DELIVER OR BUILD
  Path A (consult booked): Run discovery call → propose audit → close
  Path B (no consult): Build a lead magnet (free AI checklist for 
                       your target vertical) → share online

WEEK 4 — CLOSE OR ITERATE
  □ If proposal sent: Follow up 2× and close
  □ If no proposal: Run scorecard on 3 more opportunities
  □ Write 1 case study (even simulated for now) showing the audit value
```
