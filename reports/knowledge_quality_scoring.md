# Knowledge Quality Scoring

## Quality Scoring Factors

- Transcript completeness (length thresholds)
- Educational structure signals (`how`, `framework`, `step`, `process`)
- Risk-awareness language (`risk`, `drawdown`, `stop`, `position size`)
- Hype/scam penalties (`guaranteed`, `no risk`, `100% win`)
- Source-type weighting and bounded scoring range

## Trust Scoring (Lightweight)

- Positive signals: risk controls, checklist/rules/journal language
- Negative signals: unrealistic outcomes and hype claims
- Score bounded to keep routing stable under sparse content

## Retrieval Readiness

- Added concept tags for pre-embedding conceptual matching
- Includes ICT-aligned terms: liquidity sweep, session timing, silver bullet, market structure, displacement
