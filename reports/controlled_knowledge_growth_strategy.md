# Controlled Knowledge Growth Strategy

Date: 2026-05-10

## Objective
Grow Knowledge Brain quality without noise or unsafe automation.

## Intake Priorities
1. Funding readiness
2. Underwriting signals
3. Business credit setup
4. Onboarding friction patterns
5. Grants and opportunity fit
6. Marketing funnel performance

## Controls
- Keep `HERMES_KNOWLEDGE_AUTO_STORE_ENABLED=false`.
- Require manual review status transitions before any store action.
- Reject low-signal or duplicate notes.

## Quality checks
- Dedup key check
- Source timestamp/staleness check
- Confidence threshold tagging
- Reviewer notes required for approved items
