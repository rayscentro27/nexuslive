# Faceless Channel Operations

Date: 2026-05-11

## Channel Operating Stack
- Research queue -> Script queue -> Asset queue -> Publish queue -> Review queue

## Weekly Cadence
- Monday: trend + topic selection
- Tuesday/Wednesday: script + asset build
- Thursday: publish wave A (education)
- Friday: publish wave B (opportunity + CTA)
- Weekend: analytics + optimization retro

## Content Mix
- 40% educational explainers
- 30% tactical how-to clips
- 20% market opportunity briefs
- 10% trust-building operational updates

## Monetization Hooks
- Lead magnet CTA
- affiliate tool stack CTA
- digital template CTA
- membership waitlist CTA

## Quality Controls
- Hook test requirement before production lock
- Compliance check before publish
- No sensationalized claims without source evidence

## Performance Review
- Track by format and category cohort, not just by single post.
- Promote repeatable winners into evergreen series templates.
