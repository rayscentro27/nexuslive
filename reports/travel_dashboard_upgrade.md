# Travel Dashboard Upgrade

- Lightweight 30-second operational check is now supported by combining:
  - Workforce Office KPI strip + alerts
  - Nexus Intelligence feed
  - centralized operational snapshot endpoint
- New at-a-glance travel signals include:
  - provider health
  - workforce load
  - paper trading simulation health (win rate/outcomes/drawdown)
  - ingestion pressure
  - opportunities/grants activity
  - warning rail

This provides a phone-friendly, low-friction operational status view without enabling risky control paths.
