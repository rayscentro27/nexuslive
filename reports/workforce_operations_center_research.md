# Workforce Operations Center Research

Date: 2026-05-10
Reference reviewed: `https://github.com/pablodelucca/pixel-agents`

## Inspiration Taken
- One-worker/one-entity visual model.
- Live activity visibility as the core UX value.
- Operational status surfaced in glanceable cards.
- Lightweight, playful visual language that still communicates serious state.

## Adaptation for Nexus
- Preserve enterprise trust tone (Apple/Atlas-like light UI) rather than game-first visuals.
- Keep backend source-of-truth from real operational tables.
- Emphasize executive outcomes: queue pressure, approvals, blockers, readiness.
- Avoid heavy animations that hurt dashboard responsiveness.

## Non-Goals
- No cloning of pixel-agents experience.
- No replacement of existing AI Ops/control-center pages.
- No fake activity generation.
