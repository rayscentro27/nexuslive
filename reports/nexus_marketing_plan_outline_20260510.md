# Nexus Marketing Plan Outline (Prep)

## Ideal Client Profile
- Founder/operator with inconsistent funding results
- Small business owner needing readiness clarity before applying

## Main Pain Points
- Unsure what lenders require
- Credit/business setup gaps block approvals
- No clear sequence for funding prep

## Transformation Promise
- Move from "guessing" to a guided readiness roadmap with measurable blockers and next actions.

## Offer Stack
- Funding readiness assessment
- Credit readiness guidance
- Business setup checklist
- Opportunity/grant review add-on

## Pricing Model
- Membership + advisory tiers
- Optional performance/commission components where policy allows

## Funding Commission Framing
- Compliance-first wording; no guarantee claims; outcomes vary.

## Referral Program
- Reward qualified referrals into readiness workflows
- Auditability and transparent qualification rules

## Social Content Pillars
- Funding readiness
- Credit readiness
- Business setup
- Opportunity intelligence
- AI COO operational workflows

## Landing Page Sections
- Hero
- Problem
- Solution
- How it Works
- Features
- Compliance disclaimer
- CTA footer

## Demo Video Outline
- Current state pain
- Guided readiness workflow
- Dashboard visibility + safety
- Next-step recommendations

## First 30-Day Launch Plan
- Week 1: profile + message setup
- Week 2: educational content cadence
- Week 3: conversion experiments
- Week 4: referral and case-study cycle
