**Audience:** Nexus users and beta invitees

```
From:    The Nexus Team <goclearonline@gmail.com>
To:      [User Email]
Subject: Install the Nexus app on your phone — 30 seconds
```

---

Hi [First Name],

Nexus works best as an app on your phone.

**Open this link to get started:**

**→ https://gonexuslive.netlify.app**

---

**iPhone (Safari)**

1. Open the link above in Safari
2. Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen
3. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Tap **Add**

---

**Android (Chrome)**

1. Open the link in Chrome
2. Tap the three-dot menu in the top right
3. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"** or tap the **Install** banner at the bottom
4. Tap **Install**

---

**Desktop (Chrome or Edge)**

1. Open the link
2. Click the install icon in the address bar (or three-dot menu → "Install Nexus")
3. Click **Install**

---

**Using a different browser?**

Install works best in Safari (iPhone) and Chrome (Android and Desktop). If you're using Firefox or an in-app browser, open the link in Chrome or Safari first.

## Why This Works

| Element | Reason |
|---------|--------|
| Single URL, prominent | Eliminates confusion — one place to go |
| Numbered steps per device | Easy to follow on a small screen |
| No technical jargon | Users don't need to know what a service worker is |
| Browser specificity | Safari/Chrome requirement is the #1 support question |
| Plain reply CTA | Lower barrier than a form |
| No manifest.json / sw.js links | Those are internal verification tools, not user-facing |
| No curl commands | Wrong audience entirely |
