# Lead Capture Strategy

- Primary funnel: landing page CTA `Start Funding Readiness`.
- Secondary funnel: `View Demo` for high-intent discovery.
- Capture fields: name, business stage, funding goal, timeline, preferred contact.
- Qualification tags: startup, existing business, credit-repair-needed, grant-interest.
- Routing: high-intent leads to fast follow-up queue; others to education sequence.
- KPI baseline: conversion rate, qualified lead rate, demo-booked rate, time-to-first-response.
