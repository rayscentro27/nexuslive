## CTA Rules

- One CTA per piece of content — never stack multiple strong CTAs
- CTA always comes after the value is delivered, not before
- Comment CTAs responded to manually within 24 hours
- All CTA links go to Nexus-owned pages — no affiliate or third-party redirects
- No high-pressure language ("limited time", "before it's gone") on core CTAs
- No guaranteed outcome language in CTAs

---

## Compliance Note

CTAs must not imply guaranteed results. Avoid phrases like:
- "Get approved for funding guaranteed"
- "Build your credit score in 30 days"
- "This will get you funded"

Use instead:
- "See if your business qualifies"
- "Learn how to improve your fundability"
- "Check your funding readiness"
