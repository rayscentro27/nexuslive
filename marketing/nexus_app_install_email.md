# Nexus App Install Email Template
**Last Updated:** 2026-05-11  
**Audience:** Users and operators  
**Tone:** Premium, concise, mobile-first  
**Purpose:** Guide new users through installing Nexus as a home screen app

---

## When to Send

- After beta invite accepted
- After first sign-in
- As a follow-up for active users who haven't installed the app
- In onboarding sequence (email 2 of 5)

---

## Subject Line Options

1. `Install the Nexus app on your phone — 30 seconds`
2. `Get Nexus on your home screen`
3. `One tap away — install Nexus now`
4. `Your Nexus app is ready`

**Recommended:** `Install the Nexus app on your phone — 30 seconds`

---

## Email Body

---

**Subject:** Install the Nexus app on your phone — 30 seconds

Hi [First Name],

Nexus works best as an app on your phone.

**Open this link to get started:**

**→ https://gonexuslive.netlify.app**

Then follow the steps below for your device.

---

**iPhone (Safari)**

1. Open the link above in Safari
2. Tap the Share button (the box with an arrow at the bottom)
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Tap **Add**

The Nexus icon will appear on your home screen.

---

**Android (Chrome)**

1. Open the link above in Chrome
2. Tap the three-dot menu (top right)
3. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"** or **"Install App"**
4. Tap **Install**

If you see a banner at the bottom of the screen, tap **Install** there instead.

---

**Desktop (Chrome or Edge)**

1. Open the link above
2. Look for the install icon in the address bar (a computer screen with a down arrow)
3. Click it and select **Install**

Or: three-dot menu → **"Install Nexus"**

---

**Having trouble?**

- Make sure you're using **Safari on iPhone** or **Chrome on Android/Desktop**
- Firefox and some in-app browsers don't support installation
- If you don't see "Add to Home Screen," try refreshing the page first

Questions? Reply to this email or visit: **https://gonexuslive.netlify.app**

---

The Nexus Team  
GoClearOnline
