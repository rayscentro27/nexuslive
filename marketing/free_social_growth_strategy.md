# Nexus Free Social Growth Strategy
**Last Updated:** 2026-05-11  
**Focus:** Organic reach only — no paid ads  
**Budget:** $0 ad spend  
**Goal:** Build audience, generate leads, drive Nexus beta sign-ups

---

## Strategy Overview

The Nexus free growth strategy is built on high-value educational content published consistently across 6 platforms. Every piece of content serves two purposes: (1) build trust and topical authority, (2) drive traffic to Nexus or the free Business Funding Starter Kit.

No paid promotion. No auto-posting. All content published manually by the owner.

---

## Platform Strategies

### 1. YouTube Shorts

**Goal:** Discoverability and Shorts feed placement  
**Cadence:** 3–5 Shorts/week  
**Format:** 30–60 second educational clips  
**Why it works:** YouTube Shorts get separate distribution from long-form. Business/finance shorts get high RPM when monetized.

**Tactics:**
- Reuse long-form content — clip best 60 seconds
- Hook in first 2 seconds (statement or question, no intro)
- Single focused lesson per Short
- CTA: "Full video on the channel" or "Link in bio"
- Post same Short to Instagram Reels and TikTok same day

**Growth levers:**
- Comment responses to every comment (first 48 hours is critical)
- Pin a comment with the free resource or next video
- Use YouTube chapter markers on long-form to feed Shorts ideas

---

### 2. Instagram Reels

**Goal:** Discoverability via Reels feed + saves/shares  
**Cadence:** 4–5 Reels/week  
**Format:** 30–60 second clips (repurpose from YouTube Shorts)

**Tactics:**
- Repost YouTube Shorts as Reels (remove watermark with CapCut or similar)
- Use text overlay so content works without sound
- Stories 3–5x/week: polls, Q&A, "swipe up to join Nexus" when eligible
- Bio: clear CTA to free resource or Nexus link
- Use Linktree or Nexus direct link in bio

**Growth levers:**
- Engage in comments of larger accounts (funding, credit niche)
- Use niche hashtags: #businesscredit #businessfunding #llcsetup #entrepreneur
- Collab posts with complementary creators (non-competing)
- Save-worthy content (checklists, step-by-step) gets shared and bookmarked

---

### 3. TikTok

**Goal:** Viral reach via TikTok algorithm  
**Cadence:** 3–5 posts/week (same assets as Reels/Shorts)  
**Format:** 30–60 second clips  

**Tactics:**
- Post Reels/Shorts content same day (without YouTube/IG watermark)
- TikTok rewards consistency over virality — post daily if possible
- Use trending sounds where relevant but don't sacrifice educational value
- Engage on other finance/business creator content daily (10–15 min/day)

**Growth levers:**
- TikTok series format: "Day X of building Nexus", "Business credit step X"
- Stitch and duet responses to relevant videos
- TikTok Q&A in comments for high-engagement videos
- "Results may vary" overlay on any income or results content

---

### 4. Facebook Groups and Pages

**Goal:** Community authority, warm leads, local business reach  
**Cadence:** 3–4 posts/week on Page; daily/weekly engagement in groups  

**Tactics:**
- Facebook Page for all platform content (repurpose Reels/Shorts as Reels on Facebook)
- Join and add value in: small business groups, entrepreneur groups, funding groups
- Do not spam groups — contribute genuine answers, mention Nexus only when relevant
- Facebook Live once/month: funding Q&A or business credit workshop

**Growth levers:**
- Pin a lead capture post on the Page (free Funding Starter Kit)
- Cross-post YouTube videos to Facebook Page for SEO benefit
- Build relationship with group admins — possible guest expert slot

---

### 5. LinkedIn Authority Posts

**Goal:** B2B credibility, investor attention, professional audience  
**Cadence:** 3–4 posts/week  
**Format:** Text posts, carousels, short-form video  

**Tactics:**
- Document-style posts perform best (PDF carousels, visual slides)
- "Build in public" format: share Nexus milestones, learnings, failures
- Comment on VC, fintech, and entrepreneurship content daily
- Connect with 5–10 relevant people/week (targeted, not mass)
- Avoid link-in-post (LinkedIn suppresses reach) — put link in first comment

**Growth levers:**
- Repurpose Twitter/X threads as LinkedIn carousel posts
- Tag relevant people in posts sparingly and genuinely
- Newsletter option: LinkedIn newsletter for "Business Funding Weekly"
- LinkedIn events for virtual workshops or demos

---

### 6. X/Twitter — Build-in-Public

**Goal:** Tech-savvy audience, founder community, thought leadership  
**Cadence:** 1–3 tweets/day; 2 threads/week  
**Format:** Short posts, threads, quote-tweets  

**Tactics:**
- Tweet daily observations about building Nexus
- Weekly thread: deep dive on funding, credit, or AI for business
- Engage in founder, VC, fintech, and AI builder conversations
- No link in first tweet (algorithm suppresses it) — add in thread or reply
- Retweet with comment > plain retweet

**Growth levers:**
- Follow and engage with 10–20 aligned accounts/week
- Build-in-public series drives compounding interest
- Cross-post threads to LinkedIn as carousels
- Pin a thread that demonstrates Nexus value

## Content Repurposing Engine

One piece of content, 6 platforms:

```
[YouTube Long-form] (20 min)
  ↓ clip best 60s
[YouTube Short] → post same to [Instagram Reel] → post same to [TikTok]
  ↓ pull key points
[LinkedIn Carousel] → thread version to [X/Twitter]
  ↓ post video link
[Facebook Page] post with video embed
```

This means 1 long-form video per week = 6+ pieces of platform-native content.

## What This Strategy Does NOT Include

- Paid promotions or boosted posts
- Influencer paid partnerships
- Auto-posting or scheduling tools (manual only for now)
- Giveaways requiring platform API access
- Automated DMs or comment bots
- Any tool that violates platform terms of service
