# Onboarding Sequence

## Day 0
- Confirm business profile basics, EIN status, and funding objective.
- Collect consent for readiness analysis and data usage.

## Day 1
- Deliver funding readiness baseline with blocker list.
- Assign first action set: entity docs, banking setup, credit profile checks.

## Day 3
- Send progress check and missing-document reminders.
- Route unresolved blockers to operator queue.

## Day 7
- Deliver updated readiness score and next best funding path.
- Offer strategy session CTA.
