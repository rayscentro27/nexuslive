# ICP Definition

## Primary ICP
- Early-stage and growth-stage US business owners.
- Needs: funding readiness, business credit path, operational clarity.
- Pain points: unclear requirements, fragmented guidance, low trust in generic advice.

## Secondary ICP
- Service businesses preparing expansion with limited finance ops support.
- Wants AI-assisted operational coordination without unsafe automation.

## Disqualifiers
- Seeking guaranteed funding outcomes.
- Unwilling to provide operational/financial baseline information.
