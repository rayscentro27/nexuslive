# Pricing Strategy

- Starter: readiness assessment + roadmap + weekly check-ins.
- Growth: Starter plus workflow support and prioritized operator response.
- Operator Plus: Growth plus deeper intelligence summaries and travel-ops continuity support.

## Packaging Principles
- Price by operational outcome and responsiveness, not token usage.
- Keep clear scope boundaries (advisory + coordination, no guaranteed funding approvals).
- Preserve manual approval gates for high-risk actions.
