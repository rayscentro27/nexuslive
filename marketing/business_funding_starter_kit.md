# Business Funding Starter Kit (Free)

## What It Includes

### 1) Funding Readiness Checklist
- Business identity consistency check
- Entity/compliance check
- Banking setup check
- Documentation completeness check
- Application sequencing check

### 2) Funding Readiness Scorecard
Score each area 1-5:
- Business foundation
- Profile credibility
- Documentation readiness
- Credit readiness
- Opportunity pipeline readiness

Interpretation:
- 21-25: strong base
- 15-20: moderate, fix gaps before major applications
- <15: rebuild foundation first

### 3) Business Setup Checklist
- Entity active and verified
- EIN and core records organized
- Business website/email domain aligned
- Business contact consistency across profiles

### 4) Credit Readiness Checklist
- Review business profile records
- Resolve mismatched identifiers
- Build a low-risk application sequence
- Document denial-recovery plan before applying

### 5) Grant Tracker Concept
Columns:
- opportunity name
- source link
- eligibility status
- due date
- required assets
- submission status
- next follow-up date
