# Nexus Social Content Pillars
**Last Updated:** 2026-05-11  
**Purpose:** Define the 8 core content themes that drive all social media output

---

## Overview

Every piece of Nexus social content maps to one of these pillars. Pillars ensure consistency, build topical authority, and attract the right audience: entrepreneurs, small business owners, and side hustlers who need funding, credit, and AI leverage.

---

## Pillar 1 — Business Funding Readiness

**Theme:** How to position your business to access capital  
**Target:** Entrepreneurs who want funding but don't know where to start  
**Tone:** Practical, empowering, direct  
**Content types:** How-to videos, checklists, myth-busting  
**Sample hooks:**
- "Most businesses get denied because of THIS — not their credit score"
- "5 things lenders check before you even apply"
- "You don't need perfect credit to get business funding"

---

## Pillar 2 — Business Credit Setup

**Theme:** Building a business credit profile from scratch  
**Target:** New business owners, sole props going LLC  
**Tone:** Step-by-step, confident educator  
**Content types:** Tutorials, process walkthroughs, timelines  
**Sample hooks:**
- "Day 1 of building business credit — start here"
- "These 3 accounts report to Dun & Bradstreet without a personal guarantee"
- "Your EIN is doing nothing. Here's how to activate it"

---

## Pillar 3 — Grants and Opportunities

**Theme:** Free money for small businesses and entrepreneurs  
**Target:** Entrepreneurs who want non-dilutive capital  
**Tone:** Opportunity-focused, research-backed  
**Content types:** Grant roundups, deadline alerts, application tips  
**Sample hooks:**
- "This grant closes in 48 hours — are you eligible?"
- "3 federal grants most small businesses don't know about"
- "You don't have to pay back grants — here's where to find them"

---

## Pillar 4 — AI Business Automation

**Theme:** Using AI tools to run and scale a business with fewer hours  
**Target:** Time-strapped entrepreneurs and solopreneurs  
**Tone:** Forward-looking, tool-focused, practical  
**Content types:** Tool demos, workflow tips, AI news commentary  
**Sample hooks:**
- "I automated my entire client onboarding with AI — here's how"
- "This AI workflow saves my business 10 hours a week"
- "Stop doing this manually — AI can handle it"

---

## Pillar 5 — LLC and Business Setup

**Theme:** Structuring a real business entity correctly from day one  
**Target:** Side hustlers and new entrepreneurs  
**Tone:** Educator, protective, authority  
**Content types:** Setup guides, comparison videos, compliance basics  
**Sample hooks:**
- "LLC vs Sole Prop — which one actually protects you?"
- "You started an LLC but missed this step. Fix it now."
- "EIN, business bank account, DUNS — in this order"

---

## Pillar 6 — Side Hustle to Real Business

**Theme:** Transforming informal income into a legitimate, scalable business  
**Target:** People already making money informally  
**Tone:** Relatable, aspirational, grounded  
**Content types:** Transformation stories, milestone content, "next level" advice  
**Sample hooks:**
- "You're already in business — you just haven't made it official"
- "Going from $2k/month side hustle to $10k/month business — the real steps"
- "This one move turns your side hustle into a fundable business"

---

## Pillar 7 — Nexus Behind the Scenes / AI Workforce

**Theme:** Building Nexus in public — the AI systems, the process, the mission  
**Target:** Builders, tech-curious entrepreneurs, potential investors  
**Tone:** Transparent, founder-led, authentic  
**Content types:** Build updates, demo clips, milestone posts  
**Sample hooks:**
- "We built an AI that researches business grants 24/7 — here's how"
- "Day 47 of building Nexus — what's working and what broke"
- "9 AI agents, 1 platform — this is what we're building"

---

## Pillar 8 — Educational Trading Only

**Theme:** Trading as a skill, not a get-rich-quick scheme  
**Target:** Entrepreneurs interested in financial literacy  
**Tone:** Educational, disciplined, paper/demo only  
**Content types:** Concept explanations, market literacy, tool education  
**Compliance notes:** Paper/demo trading only. No live trade signals. No guaranteed returns. Results vary disclaimer required on all content.  
**Sample hooks:**
- "This is what a risk/reward ratio actually means in real business terms"
- "I paper traded for 90 days before risking real money — here's what I learned"
- "Understanding market cycles as a business owner"
