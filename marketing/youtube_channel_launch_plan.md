# YouTube Channel Launch Plan

Channel recommendation: **Fund Your Business**

## Positioning
Helping entrepreneurs become fundable through business credit, grants, LLC setup, funding readiness, and AI business tools.

## Audience
- New entrepreneurs (0-2 years)
- Side-hustlers transitioning to LLC
- Owners denied funding previously

## Content Pillars
1. Fundability Foundations
2. Business Credit Readiness
3. Grants and Opportunity Tracking
4. AI Tools for Small Business Operations
5. Compliance-safe funding education

## First 30-Day Launch Structure
- Week 1: 3 foundational videos + 3 Shorts
- Week 2: 2 myth-buster videos + 4 Shorts
- Week 3: 2 opportunity videos + 3 Shorts
- Week 4: 3 funnel-driven videos + 3 Shorts

## Funnel Objective
Video -> free lead magnet (Business Funding Starter Kit) -> email capture -> Nexus soft CTA.

## Brand + Format Rules
- Faceless, clean motion graphics + text-led storytelling
- Keep legal/compliance disclaimer in description and pinned comment
- Avoid income guarantees and hype claims

## KPI Targets (first 30 days)
- 1,000+ total views
- 4%+ average CTR
- 30+ lead magnet opt-ins
- 5+ qualified Nexus interest actions
