# Funding Readiness Demo v2

1. Onboarding: gather business basics, objective, and timeline.
2. Business profile: validate entity stage, banking status, and documentation health.
3. Readiness analysis: present current score and confidence range.
4. Funding blockers: show top blockers in priority order.
5. Recommendations: map each blocker to one action and owner.
6. Roadmap: 30/60/90-day readiness milestones.
7. Next actions: top five actions with urgency.
8. Executive summary: concise decision-ready close with compliance disclaimer.

Compliance: educational and advisory guidance only; no guaranteed funding outcomes.
