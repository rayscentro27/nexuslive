# Nexus Creative Engine Standard

Nexus creative output must be scored before it is queued or shown for approval. The goal is not more text. The goal is sharper hooks, stronger pain recognition, more specific business value, clearer CTA, and compliance-safe conversion into the $97 review and monthly subscription path.

## Creative Requirements

- Strong hook in the first sentence.
- Specific business/funding/credit pain.
- One practical insight the reader can use immediately.
- Clear connection to Nexus as a Credit + Funding + Opportunity operating system.
- Explicit path from free checklist to $97 review to monthly support.
- No hype-only claims.
- No guaranteed outcome language.

## Quality Threshold

Assets should score at least 75/100 before queueing. Anything below 75 is `needs_revision`. Banned claims become `blocked_compliance`.

## Rewrite Loop

Weak copy must be rewritten for:

- stronger hook
- clearer pain point
- sharper specificity
- better CTA
- stronger offer alignment
- emotional pull
- platform fit
- compliance safety

## Approval Boundary

Creative generation, scoring, queueing, and dry-run are allowed. Public posting remains Ray-approved and queue-native.
