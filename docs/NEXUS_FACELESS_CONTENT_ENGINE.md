## AI VOICE / VIDEO PRODUCTION WORKFLOW

**Step 1 — Script Generation**
- Nexus drafts script from video idea + outline
- Format: Hook (15s) → Setup (30s) → Content (body) → CTA (30s)
- Ray reviews script (5 min review)

**Step 2 — Voiceover**
- Tool: **ElevenLabs** (free tier: 10,000 chars/month)
- Voice: Professional male or female, neutral accent
- Export: MP3

**Step 3 — Video Assembly**
- Stock footage: **Pexels.com** (free, no attribution)
- B-roll: screen recordings, charts, text animations
- Editor: **CapCut** (free) or DaVinci Resolve (free)
- Template: Professional dark theme with brand colors (indigo/white)

**Step 4 — Thumbnail**
- Tool: **Canva** (free tier)
- Style: High-contrast, bold text (2–4 words), face-optional
- Test: A/B test text + color on first 5 videos

**Step 5 — Upload + Optimize**
- Title: Keyword-first format
- Description: Full script summary + affiliate links (disclosed) + lead magnet link
- Tags: 10 primary + 5 related keywords
- Chapter markers in description

**Step 6 — Repurpose**
- Extract 3 × 60s clips for YouTube Shorts
- Pull key quotes for Facebook/LinkedIn text posts
- Summarize for newsletter "Video Pick" section

**Estimated production time per video:** 2–3 hours (Ray review: 30 min)

## THUMBNAIL STYLE DIRECTION

**Style:** Bold, minimal, high contrast  
**Background:** Dark (navy or black) with accent color highlight  
**Text:** 2–4 words maximum, 80pt+ font, white or yellow  
**Graphics:** Simple icon or chart element on the right  
**No face required** — replace with: money stack, credit card, business chart, or bold number  

**Examples:**
- "$50K Funding Secret" — dark bg, money graphic, white text
- "Credit Score FIXED" — dark bg, upward chart, yellow text
- "Banks Say YES" — dark bg, checkmark, bold green accent
