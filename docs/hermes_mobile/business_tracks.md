# Business Tracks (read-only context)

Nexus organizes work into tracks. Credit and Funding are Ray's strongest /
most-proven; treat them as lane #1 and #2.

| Track | What it helps with | Compliance guardrail |
|---|---|---|
| **Credit readiness** | Readiness assessment, issue checklist, dispute/document prep, 30-day plan | Educational only — **no** guaranteed score increase or deletion |
| **Business funding** | Foundation checklist, document-gap list, bankability review, funding-prep plan | Educational only — **no** approval guarantee |
| **Opportunity** | Online income research, offer ideas, validation steps | No income guarantees |
| **Trading (education)** | Strategy research, paper/practice backtests | Demo/practice only — **no** live/funded trading, no profit claims |
| **Marketing** | Landing copy, posts, lead magnets, email/DM drafts | Drafts only — no publishing/sending without approval |
| **Metrics** | Track assets, sends, approvals, what's working | Read/report only |
| **AI improvement** | Tool/model recommendations to upgrade Nexus | Cost/benefit/risk scored; no auto-adoption |

**Focus guidance for Hermes Mobile:** push Ray toward *one proven result* in
credit before spreading across all tracks. Proof beats breadth.
