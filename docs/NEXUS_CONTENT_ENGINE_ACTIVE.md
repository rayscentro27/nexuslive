## THUMBNAIL STYLE DIRECTION

**Style:** Bold, minimal, high contrast  
**Background:** Dark (navy #0d1117 or black) with accent color highlight  
**Text:** 2–4 words max, 80pt+ font, white or #facc15 (yellow)  
**Graphics:** Simple financial icon (money stack, credit card, chart, score gauge)  
**No face required** — replace with strong typography + icon

**Template A — Big Number:**
- Background: dark navy
- Large number centered (#50K, 80+, 90)
- 3-word label below number
- Small branded corner element

**Template B — Contrast Question:**
- Dark background
- Yellow/white question text
- Supporting icon right-aligned
- "ANSWERED" or "EXPLAINED" label badge

**Template C — Before/After:**
- Split design: red left (problem) / green right (solution)
- "DENIED → FUNDED" or "0 → 80 PAYDEX"
- High contrast text both sides

**A/B test on first 5 videos:** Template A vs Template B

## AI VOICE WORKFLOW

**Step 1 — Script Generation:**
- Nexus drafts from title + outline + target length
- Format: Hook (15s) → Problem setup (30s) → Content body → CTA (30s)
- Ray reviews (5 min max)

**Step 2 — Voice Generation:**
- Tool: **ElevenLabs** (free tier: 10,000 chars/mo)
- Voice: "Adam" or "Josh" — professional, neutral male
- Export: MP3, 44.1kHz

**Step 3 — Video Assembly:**
- Stock footage: **Pexels.com** (free, no attribution)
- B-roll: financial charts, business settings, text overlays
- Editor: **CapCut** (free) or DaVinci Resolve
- Template: Dark professional theme (navy/white/gold)

**Step 4 — Subtitles:**
- Auto-generated in CapCut
- Style: Bold white, drop shadow, 80% opacity background strip

**Step 5 — Thumbnail:**
- Tool: **Canva** (free tier)
- Template: Saved for consistency

**Step 6 — Upload + Optimize:**
- Title: Keyword-first (target keyword in first 3 words)
- Description: Full summary + affiliate links (disclosed) + lead magnet
- Tags: 10 primary + 5 related
- Chapter markers at 2-min intervals
- End screen: Subscribe + Watch Next (3 days after upload)

**Step 7 — Repurpose:**
- Extract 3 × 60s clips → YouTube Shorts
- Pull 3 quotes → Facebook/LinkedIn text posts
- Summarize → Newsletter "Video Pick" section

**Estimated time per video:** 2–3 hours total (Ray review: 30 min)
