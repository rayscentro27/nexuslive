**Headline:** What Does Your Score Actually Mean?

> Your Funding Readiness Score (0–100) is calculated across 6 dimensions:

| Dimension | Weight | What Lenders Care About |
|-----------|--------|-------------------------|
| Business Credit Profile | 25% | PAYDEX, Experian Business, trade lines |

> **Score 61–80:** Good position. Your report identifies the final gaps to close.  
> **Score 81–100:** Strong position. Your report identifies the best lenders and optimal timing.
