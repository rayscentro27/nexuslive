## Compliance-Safe Language Guide

### Approved phrases

- "Explore tools that may help you understand your business funding profile."
- "Review resources that can help you prepare before applying."
- "Funding decisions depend on lender criteria."
- "This does not guarantee funding or approval."
- "This is a preparation step, not a guarantee of approval."
- "This may be one option to research."
- "One option to consider as you prepare."
- "A tool some business owners use to review their profile."

### Prohibited phrases — do not use

| Prohibited | Why |
|------------|-----|
| "Guaranteed funding" | Misleading — lenders make their own decisions |
| "Guaranteed approval" | Cannot guarantee lender decisions |
| "Instant funding" | Misleading about timeline |
| "Lenders will approve you" | Cannot promise lender outcomes |
| "Fix your credit" | Regulated language; implies guaranteed credit improvement |
| "Remove negative items guaranteed" | Regulated; makes unverifiable promise |
| "Guaranteed credit improvement" | Regulated; cannot guarantee credit outcomes |
| "Guaranteed business credit results" | Regulated; cannot guarantee results |
| "Get funded" (as guarantee) | Misleading if implies certainty |
| "We helped X get $Y in funding" (without verification) | Testimonial compliance risk |

### Disclosure language (for all CTAs)

The following disclosure must accompany any affiliate CTA once live links are approved:

```
[Disclosure: This link may be an affiliate link. If you click and purchase
or sign up, we may receive a commission. This does not affect our
recommendations or the price you pay. Funding decisions depend on lender
criteria and are not guaranteed.]
```
