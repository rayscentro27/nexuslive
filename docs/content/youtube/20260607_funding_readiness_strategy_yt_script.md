**Section 1: Investor-Grade Financials (1:30-3:00)**  
*(Visual: Animated financial charts)*  
- Why 90% of decks fail: Missing unit economics and scalability triggers.

**Section 2: Traction Stacking (3:00-5:00)**  
*(Visual: Progress bar filling with milestones)*  
- Pre-revenue? Use "proxy metrics": Letters of intent, waitlists, or pilot partnerships.  
- Case Study: How a SaaS founder leveraged 500 beta users to close a $2M seed round.  
- **Red Flag:** Never label early customers as "proof of concept"—reframe as "validation phase."  

**Section 3: Legal Fort Knox (5:00-6:30)**  
*(Visual: Shield icon protecting documents)*  
- 3 deal-killers: Unclean cap tables, missing IP assignments, vague shareholder agreements.  
- **Action:** Audit your legal docs NOW—use tools like Clerky or Atlas (cost: <$1k).  
- Investor Insight: VCs spend 73% of due diligence on legal risk.  

**Section 4: Team Credibility Hacks (6:30-8:00)**  
*(Visual: Puzzle pieces forming a team)*

- Data: Teams with technical + commercial co-founders raise 30% more capital.  

**Section 5: The Due Diligence Dossier (8:00-9:30)**  
*(Visual: Checklist with green ticks)*

- **Psychological Hack:** Share your "risks" upfront—it builds trust and disarms objections.  
- Tool Stack: Dropbox Vault + DocSend for secure sharing.
