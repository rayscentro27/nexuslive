**THE INTELLIGENCE**  
Tiers dictate *who* funds you and *at what cost*. Here’s the breakdown:

- **Tier 3 (<650):** Survival mode. High-collateral demands, merchant cash advances (effective APRs 40-150%).  
*The leverage? Tier jumps compound.* Moving from Tier 3 to Tier 2 slashes your cost of capital by 60%. Tier 1 to Tier 2? Another 30-50% savings.
