**THE INTELLIGENCE: Tier Warfare**  
Business credit operates in three tiers:  
1.  **Starter (0-70):** High-risk. Net-30 vendors, secured cards. Limits under $50k.  
2.  **Established (71-80):** Prime territory. Unsecured cards, SBA loans. $50k-$250k access.  
3.  **Elite (81+):** Banks court *you*. 0% teaser rates, 7-figure lines. Minimal personal liability.  
**The unlock?** Lenders reward *predictable leverage*. Use 10-30% of limits, pay early, and layer tradelines (net-30, then revolving). Tier jumps happen at 10-point intervals—plan your moves.
