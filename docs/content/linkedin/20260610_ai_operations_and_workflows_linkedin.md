# LinkedIn Post — AI operations and workflows

**Your AI Strategy is Meaningless Without This "Missing Middle" Layer.**  

Most leaders treat AI like a magic wand:  
• Grand visions, pilot projects... then radio silence.  
• Teams drown in fragmented tools and duct-tape workflows.  
• Results stall because *using* AI daily is chaotic, not controlled.  

**AI doesn't scale on models alone. It scales on OPERATIONS.**  
Forget just strategy or tech. Sustainable AI needs a hardened workflow layer:  

1.  **Orchestrate, Don't Isolate:** Connect your AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.) *seamlessly* into existing business apps (Slack, CRM, Drive). No more copy-paste purgatory.  
2.  **Govern the Chaos:** Standardize prompts, audit outputs, control access. Ensure quality & compliance *before* things break.  
3.  **Humans-in-the-Loop, Optimized:** Design workflows where AI handles grunt work, but critical thinking and approvals flow effortlessly to your team.  

Without this operational backbone, even the smartest AI becomes shelfware.
