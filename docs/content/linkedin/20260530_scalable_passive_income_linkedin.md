# LinkedIn Post — scalable passive income

**Most passive income advice is a lie. Here’s why.**  

Everyone wants income that grows while they sleep. But chasing side hustles like Uber, Etsy shops, or affiliate links usually means trading *more* time for *more* work. It’s active income disguised as passive. You hit a ceiling fast.  

Scalable passive income isn’t about hustling harder. It’s about designing systems that multiply value without multiplying effort.  

Here’s the framework that actually works:  
1. **Build Systems, Not Tasks:** Replace your direct involvement with automated workflows, software, or leveraged assets (like IP or digital products).  
2. **Focus on Leverage Multipliers:** Use audiences (email lists, communities), partnerships, or scalable platforms to amplify reach *without* proportional time investment.  
3. **Engineer for Automation:** Prioritize income streams that require near-zero maintenance after launch (e.g., evergreen courses, SaaS, royalties).  

True passive scale means your revenue grows exponentially while your input plateaus.
