# LinkedIn Post — AI operations and workflows

**Most AI projects fail because owners skip operations. (Not the tech.)**  

We get excited about ChatGPT pilots or custom models. But scaling? That’s where things stall.  
Teams juggle disconnected prompts, manual data checks, and chaotic approval chains.  
It feels like building a race car... only to push it down the highway. Exhausting.  

**Here’s the shift: AI isn’t just a tool. It’s an employee needing workflows.**  
My clients win by designing *how* AI works, not just *what* it does:  

1.  **Map the Handoffs:** Where does human judgment *actually* intervene? Define those gates *before* errors snowball.  
2.  **Automate the Mundane:** Stop babysitting inputs/outputs. Use simple scripts to prep data, trigger AI, and route results instantly.  
3.  **Monitor, Don’t Micromanage:** Track key metrics (accuracy drift, latency, cost per task) – not every single output.  

This turns pilots into profit centers. Less firefighting, more flow.
