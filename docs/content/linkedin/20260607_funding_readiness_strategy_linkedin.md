# LinkedIn Post — funding readiness strategy

**Most founders think they're "funding ready." 98% aren't.**  

Investors reject great ideas daily, not because the idea is bad, but because the founder *wasn't truly prepared*. You scramble to pull documents together last minute, pitch decks feel disjointed, and crucial financials look shaky under scrutiny. This lack of readiness kills deals and momentum.  

True funding readiness isn't about having a fancy deck; it's strategic alignment. Here’s the core framework:  

1.  **Investor Fit First:** Are you targeting the *right* investors who genuinely care about your space and stage? Spray-and-pray wastes everyone's time.  
2.  **Financial Foresight:** Can you instantly defend every assumption in your model and show a clear, believable path to key milestones? Vague projections scream risk.  
3.  **Narrative Cohesion:** Does your story – from problem to solution to traction to team – flow logically and *prove* you can execute? Gaps create doubt.  

Master these, and you move from "hoping" for funding to confidently *commanding* it.
