# LinkedIn Post — business credit tiers and funding

**Business credit tiers aren’t just scores – they’re your funding roadmap. 🗺️**

Most founders hit a wall. They need capital to scale, but traditional lenders say "no." They chase personal guarantees or high-interest loans, feeling stuck and frustrated. The problem? They’re playing in the wrong credit tier – or don’t know tiers *exist*.

Your business credit isn’t one monolithic number. It operates in distinct tiers, each unlocking specific funding types as you climb:

1.  **🔒 Tier 1 (Entry):** Vendor/net 30 accounts, small store cards. *Goal:* Establish initial files with D&B/Experian.  
2.  **📈 Tier 2 (Growth):** Higher-limit retail cards, fleet cards, smaller bank lines. *Goal:* Build strong PAYDEX/Intelliscore, separate from personal credit.  
3.  **🚀 Tier 3 (Established):** Major bank cards, high-limit LOC, SBA loans, prime rates. *Goal:* Access substantial unsecured capital for big moves.  

Moving up requires strategy: consistent reporting, proper utilization, and knowing *which* tradelines build which tier. Skipping steps keeps funding out of reach.
