# X Posts — PAYDEX score optimization

Here are 5 high-impact X posts on PAYDEX score optimization for entrepreneurs and business owners:

POST 1:  
Your PAYDEX score isn't just about paying bills—it's about timing. Paying 30 days early gives you 80 points. Paying on due date? Just 60. Early payments unlock better credit terms. Are you leaving points on the table?

POST 2:  
Most biz owners don't know: Your PAYDEX score weighs recent payments 2X heavier than older ones. One late payment 6 months ago hurts less than one last month. The fix? Flawless payments for 90 days to rebound fast.

POST 3:  
Contrary to popular belief, maxing out your PAYDEX (80+) can backfire. Lenders see 85-100 as "too perfect" and assume you're not using credit strategically. The sweet spot? 75-84. Are you over-optimizing?

POST 4:  
Small suppliers report faster than big vendors. A $500 on-time payment to your local supplier boosts your PAYDEX faster than a $50k payment to Amazon. Build credit smarter—who are you prioritizing?

POST 5:  
Your PAYDEX score refreshes every 30 days—not instantly. That "urgent" payment you just made won't show for weeks. Pro tip: Schedule payments 45 days before you need financing. When did you last check your report?
