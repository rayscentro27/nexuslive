# X Posts — business credit tiers and funding

Here are 5 high-impact X posts on business credit tiers and funding:

POST 1:  
Business credit tiers aren't just about limits—they're access keys. Tier 1 lenders (banks) offer lowest rates but highest scrutiny. Tier 3 (alt lenders) cost more but fund faster. Smart entrepreneurs use both strategically. Which tier is fueling your growth right now?

POST 2:  
The dirty secret of business credit: Your personal score still matters. Even with an EIN, most lenders peek at your FICO until you hit $100K+ revenue. Build both simultaneously or stay stuck in startup purgatory.

POST 3:  
"Pre-approved" business credit offers are traps. They bait you with high limits then slash them after hard pulls. Real business credit builds gradually through trade lines and vendor accounts. Slow = fast in this game.

POST 4:  
Funding paradox: The businesses that qualify for the best rates often need money least. Solution? Establish credit lines BEFORE you need them. The richest tool is the unused one—applies to capital too.

POST 5:  
Stop obsessing over credit scores. Lenders care more about banking patterns. Consistent $10K+ monthly deposits get you farther than a 750 score with empty accounts. Money flows to where money moves.
